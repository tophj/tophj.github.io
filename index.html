About Me

Hello. My name is Chris Jones and I’m a junior software engineer located in Cambridge MD.

I enjoy both front and backend development, and have experience doing so! My previous stack was comprised of Linux, Apache, Neo4j a NoSQL graph database, and Python.

In the past I’ve developed applications for the University of Virginia Department of Chemistry, which were used by over 1600 students a semester; web crawlers for data retrieval and analysis for Battelle Memorial Institute; and a built from scratch BitTorrent client in C for my capstone project at Virginia Tech.

Some of my personal projects include a python-based recreation of the enigma machine, a Google chrome extension for automatically censoring TV spoilers, and a mock up of the Google homepage I contributed to The Odin Project, an open source curriculum for learning web development.

If you would like to contact me, feel free to connect with me on linkedin , or send me an email.
